Time Attack Mode

Race against your best time, or against the developers and testers!

In Time Attack, you’re locked to Gear 3, and all Item Boxes are replaced with high-power Ring Boxes. There’s nothing faster—give it your all!

Position will give you 15 seconds to set up a good start before the real countdown begins. You can launch off whenever you’re ready, and the timer will start once you begin the lap.

Recording a good time can earn Bronze, Silver, and Gold medals; check the par time on Course Select, or above the timer in-game.

  • Replay: Relive your greatest runs, or peek the staff ghost strategies.
  • Ghosts: Enable ghosts if you want to play for pace, or disable ghosts if you want to chill out alone.
  • During Time Attack, the Y button no longer triggers Ring Shooter—instead, it will instantly restart the round.
  • After completing a certain challenge, press R after selecting a course for a high-pressure challenge…
