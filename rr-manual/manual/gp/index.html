Grand Prix Mode

A five-round tournament to see who’s the best! Aim for 1st place in every lap!

Before starting, select a difficulty level from the Grand Prix Menu. Then head to “Cup Select”, and choose the tournament you want to enter. Only “Ring Cup” is available right away; qualify in each cup to unlock the next!

The Road To Mastery!

“Relaxed” difficulty is slower and more forgiving. “Intense” is faster, and it’ll push you harder as you get better.

If you’re feeling overwhelmed or understimulated, try another difficulty; the best way to learn is a balance of high and low pressure.

Don’t be afraid to replay previous cups, or practice tough circuits in “Match Race”. Mastering the Ring Racer is one thing—mastering the tracks is another!

Position

Set yourself up for success during the pre-race countdown. During Position, a barrier will appear over the finish line. Players that make contact with it will be Faulted until the race starts.

Pass the line AFTER the countdown ends, but BEFORE everyone else, and you’ll receive the “First Blood” boost; use it to get a head start or take shortcuts early!

Position lasts longer when there are more players in the race. Use the lamps at the top of the screen to find the perfect timing.

Ranking

You’ll be ranked at the end of every race based on your overall performance. Every lap counts!

  • Your EXP Bonus increases each time you cross a Checkpoint or complete a lap. Stay up front to collect more points!
  • You get one point of Ring Bonus for each Ring you’re holding when the race finishes.
    • Every 20 Rings you smuggle past the finish will net you an extra life.
    • (If you’re being trailed by the Self-Propelled Bomb, we’ll spot you 20 points!)

Bonus Round

In Bonus Rounds, you’ve got a limited time to smash every Prison Egg in the arena. Collect Blue Spheres to speed up your Ring Racer, and use Gachaboms and the Insta-Whip to bust your TARGETs!

Breaking every Prison Egg in a Bonus Round awards an extra life. They also affect your overall grade.

Rival Racer

After the first round, you’ll see the Rival tag on a certain competitor; they’re favored to win the tournament, and they’ll be driving their best to beat you. Take out your Rival to get an edge on the competition!

Every character has different Rivals. Try Grand Prix with a variety of characters, and maybe you’ll see some new faces on the track…?

Final Results

At the end of the Grand Prix, all of your achievements will be tallied up into a final overall rank.

  • Placement Bonus (150pts): Your final standing after the 5th Round.
  • EXP Bonus (100pts): Your standing at each Checkpoint and fresh lap.
  • Prison Bonus (100pts): Every Prison broken during the Bonus Round.
  • Ring Bonus (50pts): Total Rings carried after the end of each race.
  • Continue Penalty (-40/ea): Stay alive for bonus points!

Score 140 points for Rank D, 200 points for Rank C, 280 points for Rank B, and 340 points for the coveted Rank A!

High ranks can be tough! Don’t be afraid to move on; sometimes a fresh locale to practice in makes all the difference on the next try.
