What Happens After The End Of The World?

The cunning scientist, Dr. Ivo Robotnik, was a very smart man. But sometimes the smartest people are the greatest fools of all. In his latest, most dangerous plot to take over the world, he had learned his grandfather wanted to destroy it! It was only with the help of his sworn foes that he could stop that terrifying plan from coming to be.

In order to power his many machines, Robotnik used unethical power sources like small animals. It took being put in danger, but now the self-centered scientist has started reconsidering these evil deeds. To solve this problem, he began working on a new power source with somebody used to freeing these animals and stopping his plans.

This was the smart fox with two tails. He was born as Miles Prower, but chose for himself to be the hero, Tails! If anyone on the planet Earth was as intelligent as Dr. Robotnik, it would be him. As enemies, they impressed each other - but working together, they could accomplish incredible things.

Both of them had names given by the speed-of-sound hedgehog, Sonic. The blue blur would probably always think of Robotnik as the rotten egg, “Eggman”. But maybe he could use his genius to help people, too.

Their new power source was the High Voltage Ring! With over 80,000 Rings combined, there was a lot of potential. They could power the world, not just his robots. But this Ring was dangerous! It needs real-world testing to make sure it doesn’t explode.

But Robotnik began doing evil deeds in the first place because of his childish love of fun. And though Tails is very smart, he is still young as well. So the test of this power source would be a tournament featuring a very special type of go-kart!

These go-karts are called Ring Racers. Can Robotnik and his ex-enemies master them?
