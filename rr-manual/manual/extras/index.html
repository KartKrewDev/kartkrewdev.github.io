Extras Menu

When you’re looking for a break from standard racing, you might find something useful here.

Addons

Customize your experience with user-created racers, followers, courses and gamemodes. Visit srb2.org/addons to browse for community creations!

Tutorial

Help Eggman and Tails test their new Ring Racers! If you took an early EXIT, or just want a refresher course, you can visit any part of the adventure from here.

Egg TV

Watch your greatest victories and most memorable defeats—or peek at a netgame opponent’s racing line. Replays you save at the end of races will collect here for safekeeping.

Statistics

Keep track of all the details of your adventure! See collected Spray Cans, Medals, and some bonus trivia about your time spent racing.

Password

Input special codes to unlock various goodies!
