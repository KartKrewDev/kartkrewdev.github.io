Lightning Shield

Race Only | ATK ★★ | DEF ★★★ | SPD ★★★

Controls

L

Charge up, then destroy shield! (small blast, boost forward)

Details

Powers up your Tether, pulls in nearby Rings, and protects your Ring count from a single hit. Attract yourself to your opponents like a magnet, then detonate to shock them and zip ahead!

When YOU'VE got it... When THEY'VE got it...
  • A permanent Tether boost is great for overtaking on many courses—but if it’s full of tight bends or Trick Panels, you might need to change your approach.
  • While the Lightning Shield is charging, you’re immune to almost all damage, including track hazards. Should you hold on for an emergency…?
  • The Lightning Shield’s passive electromagnetism will disrupt nearby rings, knocking them out of formation to trail behind you.
  • Aggressive play can bait the leader into charging early; take your shot once their shield is blown!
  • If there’s a Lightning Shield user on your back, you’ll be able to see their distinct charged-up Tether; you’ve got time to plan your defense.
  • Lightning Shield needs time to charge before it detonates; look for the sparks and listen for the sound, then weave!
