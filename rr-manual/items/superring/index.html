Super Ring

Race Only | ATK - | DEF ★ | SPD ★★

Controls

L

Use, gaining Rings

Details

The Super Ring gives up all offense and defense for a frontrunner’s greatest dream—more speed. Grants 20 Rings on use, spending any extras, and pushes you forward.

When YOU'VE got it... When THEY'VE got it...
  • When you’re in 1st place with 0 Rings, your next Item Roulette will be packed with extra Super Rings. If you’re pushing for speed or outrunning the SPB, this can be great. If your pursuers are taking aim, well…
  • If you’re in the lead, your opponents are often closer to danger than you are. If you spend Rings, it can force them to follow suit.
  • When you’re far away from the leader, the sudden burst of speed from a Super Ring might trigger a Sonic Boom; when you see Subsonic wind, look for an opportunity to take a shortcut!
  • If your opponent has a Super Ring, they don’t have defensive items—push them with aggressive tethers and Ring usage, and you might follow them forward!
  • Rings give the frontrunner extra speed variance and handling power. Don’t let them juke your items! Keep a steady hand and fire when they’re forced to slow down, like on tight corners.
