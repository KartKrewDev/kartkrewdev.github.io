Chain yourself!

Details

Disguised as an dropped Sneaker, the Stone Shoe chains the victim to a boulder on contact; they’ll slow down, but anyone who touches the boulder pays out Amps for both of you!

When YOU'VE got it... When THEY'VE got it...
  • Stone Shoe can get you way ahead if your pursuers are close together. Place your traps where the next ten seconds will cause maximum mayhem.
  • Keen-eyed players won’t be fooled by a grey Sneaker; don’t give them the chance to see it or steer away!
  • In close quarters or tight hallways, you can often chain yourself to shake a single pursuer. Careful, though; you’ll slow down to do it, and the pack could come calling!
  • Stone Shoe offers zero direct defense. The threat of a medium-distance missile shot can totally defuse the leader’s plans.
  • Don’t telegraph your steering direction too early, or the leader could trap you between a wall and the chain!
  • Got chained? You’ll receive some Amps as compensation; activate Overdrive and ruin somebody’s day!
