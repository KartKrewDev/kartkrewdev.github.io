Toxomister

ATK ★ | DEF ★★ | SPD ★★

Controls

L

Set as a trap
↑ + L

Lob forward

Details

The Toxomister is a static trap that spews toxic gas, slowing down its victims and draining their Rings.

When YOU'VE got it... When THEY'VE got it...
  • A Sonic Boom will blow Toxomister away; it’s useless on downward inclines or sections with tons of Sneaker Panels.
  • After it’s been out for a while, the Toxomister runs out of gas. Snatch it by driving into it, and it’ll be full again when it’s redeployed.
  • Toxomister prevents Ring usage and offers no defenses, but if you’re being pressured, a quick swerve can catch pursuers off guard.
  • A leader holding Toxomister is almost completely defenseless; as long as you’re not riding their bumper, a slingshot overtake is nearly guaranteed.
  • An inert Toxomister can be shot or bumped to destroy it, but even an active Toxomister can be defused with a missile.
  • If you’re choking on a cloud, route for a pile of Rings to restock, or aim for Sneaker and Dash Panels to blow the gas away!
